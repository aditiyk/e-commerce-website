Our Personal Fashion Tips!

1. Make sure you have reliable wardrobe staples: an iconic little black dress, a pair of jeans that fit perfectly, a classic blazer, simple T-shirts and button-downs in neutral colors.

2. Make sure your clothes fit perfectly. One trick to making any item of clothing look amazing is to hire a good tailor. Tailored clothing not only looks polished, but it also feels more comfortable.

3. Find your personal style.

4. Become a better shopper. Learning how to shop for exactly what you want will help you avoid filling your closet full of items you never wear.

It is never too late to start experimenting with fashion!
